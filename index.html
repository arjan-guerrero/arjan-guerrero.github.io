about

Media Forensis research aims to identify media’s own material, aesthetic and discoursive bias, implementing investigative aesthetics to produce fiction-science in an exploration of evidence as hyperstition.

Media Forensis is a platform where several agents become nodes of temporary assemblies with the purpose of exposing the more revealing hidden agencies of each assembly. Usually the outputs include artworks that present the aesthetics of those agencies, but written essays or tools may result as well.

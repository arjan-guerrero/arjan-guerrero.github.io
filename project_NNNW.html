New New New World

Across a virtual reality, within the latent space of the Big GAN algorithm, the territory of the New New New World unfolds. We're curating it into existence

This is the exploration, taxonomization, and the making real of a New New New World of objects. By means of a series of artworks for human-sentience training and a novel image dataset for AI training (Machine Learning), we’re curating its way into existence.
As an echo of the exploration of the New World in the XV and XVI c., we are navigating the New New New World (NNNW) under a different paradigm: this is a world to be more produced than discovered, to be traced within the future more than in the past, and to be regarded as a hyperstition more than as a myth.

We're exploring the NNNW in first-hand across the Latent Space of the BigGAN—the AI image synthesizer that knows better than every other AI what the world looks like through a digital camera.

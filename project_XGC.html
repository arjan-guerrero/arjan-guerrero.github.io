XEROGRAMMAR CASE

A failed attempt to read and write the writen language of Kyo the photocopier.

In the late XXI century and within a full Age of Entanglement characterized by the profound interconnection amongst material and immaterial things, there appears the (human) problem of the technological disconnection (the becoming autonomous of technology). As part of a general scientific reaction, at Media Forensis we traced the material origins of the artificial intelligences' linguistic disconnection. Founding Kyo was a revelation.
